Step 1. Importing CSV files into Power BI Desktop


Find and connect the dataset folder.

1.     First you need to have the data save on your device.

2.     Open Poer BI desktop

3.     Click on Get Data ribbon. 

4.     Select Text or CSV format and click Connect.

5.     Choose “Master” and click Open.

6.     When the preview window comes click on transform and change the table name to Suicide Data.

7.     Click on apply and save.
